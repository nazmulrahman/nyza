Project Overview

The Solar Panel Cleaning Robot is a fully functional mobile system engineered to clean solar panels efficiently without causing any surface damage. Designed with precision and care, it can climb and operate on panels at various angles, ensuring effective dust and debris removal while maintaining maximum solar output.

This robot combines innovative mechanical design with practical field requirements to deliver a durable, lightweight, and easy-to-maintain solution. It significantly reduces the need for manual cleaning, lowers operational costs, and ensures consistent energy production. The system is ideal for large-scale solar farms and commercial rooftop installations in dusty environments.

Key Features

Rotatable Push-in L-fitting

Ensures free rotation of the water supply pipe, preventing kinks. This guarantees continuous water flow for efficient wet cleaning, adapting to panel angles without hindering the robot's smooth operation.

Linear Actuator

Precisely lifts cleaning brushes, allowing tracks to climb over panel edges and obstacles. This prevents damage to brushes and panels, ensuring versatile, safe navigation on varied solar installations.

BLDC Motor coupled with a shaft and belt-pulley

Efficiently rotates cleaning brushes with consistent power. This optimizes debris removal, ensuring high-performance cleaning, durability, and energy efficiency for the robot's extended operation.
