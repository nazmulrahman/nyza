Featured Projects

Military Robotics

Autonomous Tank Robot

OVERVIEW

An advanced autonomous ground robot designed for battlefield operations and surveillance, capable of remote operation from anywhere in the world.

PROJECT BRIEF

The system integrates ROS2, RTK GPS, LiDAR, 3D cameras, and IMU sensors to enable real-time mapping, navigation, and situational awareness. It supports both indoor and outdoor SLAM for autonomous mobility across complex terrains.

ROLE

Lead Mechatronics Engineer, Robotics Programmer

METHOD

From mechanical design and prototyping to software development and testing, we handled the entire development cycle. The robot features a modular structure with AI-driven perception, reliable communication, and robust performance across challenging military terrains.

Renewable Energy Robotics

Solar Panel Cleaning Robot

OVERVIEW

An innovative solution was required to climb solar panels without hurting the cleaning brushes.

PROJECT BRIEF

Designed a mobile robot with all the requirements given by the client and made a lot of modifications based on my experience with robots. All custom parts were built with design for manufacturing considerations.

ROLE

Lead mechanical designer, Mechanical Designer

METHOD

After some intensive research, the product concept was developed, and then we tried to list down the off-the-shelf components. The parts were modeled in Fusion 360, and motors, mounts, transmissions, bearings, brackets, and fasteners were gradually selected according to the needs.
